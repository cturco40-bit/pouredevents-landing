HOW IT WORKS
01
CREATE & INVITE
Event hosts create the event, set ticket tiers and pricing, and invite agents by email. Agents accept, pay their listing fee, and upload their portfolio.
02
TASTE & ORDER
Attendees buy tickets, enter the event with a passcode, browse wines on their phone by supplier table, and place orders during the tasting.
03
CONNECT & CLOSE
Agents unlock buyer details, confirm orders, and follow up directly. Orders stay open for 48 hours after the event — no missed opportunities.
